Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

The Atal Pension Yojana (APY) is a pension scheme for workers in the unorganized sector, designed to ensure a guaranteed pension after 60 years of age.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18 to 40 years
  • Must have a bank account
  • Can contribute a fixed amount monthly until retirement

Benefits

  • Guaranteed pension of ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 after retirement.
  • Government contributes 50% of the user's contribution.

How to Apply

You can apply for APY by visiting your nearest bank or through online banking services. Apply for APY Online
