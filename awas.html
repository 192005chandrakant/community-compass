Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana aims to provide affordable housing to the urban poor, ensuring housing for all by 2022.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Should belong to the Economically Weaker Section (EWS), Low-Income Group (LIG), or Middle-Income Group (MIG).
  • Must not own a pucca house anywhere in India.

Benefits

  • Subsidized home loan interest rates for eligible applicants.
  • Financial assistance for construction or purchase of a house.
  • Access to additional incentives for women, SC/ST, and disabled individuals.

How to Apply

Interested applicants can apply through the official PMAY website or designated banks. Apply for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Online
