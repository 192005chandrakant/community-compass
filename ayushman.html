Ayushman Bharat Yojana (PMJAY)

Ayushman Bharat Yojana, also known as the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), is a national health protection scheme that aims to provide health insurance to low-income families and vulnerable sections of society.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Families identified under the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) database are eligible for the scheme.
  • No age limit or family size restriction.
  • Specifically targeted at vulnerable rural and urban populations.
