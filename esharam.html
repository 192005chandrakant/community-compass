e-Shram Yojana

e-Shram is a national database aimed at unorganized workers, which provides them with social security benefits and ensures that they receive the necessary assistance in times of need.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Any unorganized worker aged between 16 to 59 years.
  • Workers in the unorganized sector including construction workers, domestic workers, and agricultural workers.
  • Must not be a member of any organized sector social security scheme.

Benefits

  • Access to various social security benefits like insurance, pensions, and financial aid during emergencies.
  • Registration on the e-Shram portal ensures a unique identity for workers.
  • Eligibility for various government schemes and benefits.

How to Apply

Workers can register on the e-Shram portal either online or by visiting designated centers. Apply for e-Shram Yojana Online
