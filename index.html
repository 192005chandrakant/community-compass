Welcome to Maharashtra Migrant Worker Schemes

Find out about the schemes and benefits available for migrant workers in Maharashtra.

Available Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PMSYM)

A pension scheme for unorganized workers. Provides a fixed monthly pension after retirement.

Read More
Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

A pension scheme for workers in the unorganized sector. Ensures a guaranteed pension after 60 years of age.

Read More
Maharashtra Migrant Worker Welfare Scheme

A state-specific scheme aimed at providing financial and healthcare assistance to migrant workers.

Read More
e-Shram

A national database for unorganized workers to provide them with social security benefits.

Read More
Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

An affordable housing scheme for economically weaker sections, including migrant workers.

Read More
Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

Guarantees 100 days of employment in rural areas for unskilled labor, benefiting migrant workers.

Read More
Ayushman Bharat

A health insurance scheme providing free treatment for low-income workers at empaneled hospitals.

Read More
Public Distribution System (PDS)

Provides subsidized food grains to low-income families, including migrant workers.

Read More
National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP)

Provides financial assistance to the elderly, widows, and persons with disabilities among migrant workers.

Read More
