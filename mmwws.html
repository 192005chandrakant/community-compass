Maharashtra Migrant Worker Welfare Scheme

The Maharashtra Migrant Worker Welfare Scheme aims to provide financial and healthcare assistance to migrant workers, ensuring their welfare and social security.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Migrant workers who have migrated from other states to Maharashtra for employment.
  • Workers in various sectors including construction, agriculture, and industry.
  • Must possess a valid identity proof and employment details.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance during emergencies and health issues.
  • Access to healthcare facilities and insurance coverage.
  • Support for children's education and skill development programs.

How to Apply

Eligible workers can apply for the scheme through designated government offices or online. Apply for Maharashtra Migrant Worker Welfare Scheme Online
