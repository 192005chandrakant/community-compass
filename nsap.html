National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP)

The National Social Assistance Programme provides financial assistance to elderly, widows, and persons with disabilities, ensuring social security for the vulnerable sections of society.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Aged 60 years or above for the elderly pension.
  • Must be a widow or a person with a disability for respective benefits.
  • Income should be below the defined poverty line.

Benefits

  • Monthly financial assistance for the elderly, widows, and persons with disabilities.
  • Support aimed at enhancing the quality of life for beneficiaries.
  • Encouragement for social inclusion and protection.

How to Apply

Interested individuals can apply through the local Social Welfare Department or online. Apply for National Social Assistance Programme Online
