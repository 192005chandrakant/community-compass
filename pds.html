Public Distribution System (PDS)

The Public Distribution System aims to provide subsidized food grains to low-income families, ensuring food security for all.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a resident of India.
  • Households should fall under the Below Poverty Line (BPL) or Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) categories.
  • Must possess a valid ration card issued by the government.

Benefits

  • Access to subsidized food grains such as rice, wheat, and sugar.
  • Support for nutritional needs of families.
  • Promotion of food security among economically weaker sections.

How to Apply

Eligible families can apply for a ration card through the local rationing office or online. Apply for Public Distribution System Online
