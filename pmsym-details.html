Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PMSYM)

The Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PMSYM) is a pension scheme aimed at providing financial security to workers in the unorganized sector. This scheme ensures that workers will receive a pension after retirement.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18 to 40 years
  • Monthly income should be less than ₹15,000
  • Should not be a member of the EPF/NPS/ESIC

Benefits

  • Fixed monthly pension of ₹3,000 after retirement.
  • Contributions range from ₹55 to ₹200, depending on age.
  • Government co-contributes to the pension fund.

How to Apply

Workers can enroll by visiting the nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or registering online through the official PMSYM portal.
